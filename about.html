About ZimSports League

Empowering sports development across Zimbabwe through innovative technology and passionate community building.

Our Mission

To revolutionize sports league management in Zimbabwe by providing accessible, efficient, and modern tools that empower sports administrators, coaches, and players to focus on what matters most - the game.

We believe that every sports organization, regardless of size, deserves professional-grade management tools to help develop talent and grow the sports community.

Our Vision

To be the leading sports management platform in Africa, fostering the growth of grassroots sports and helping discover the next generation of sporting talent across the continent.

We envision a Zimbabwe where every community league, school team, and professional organization has the tools to succeed and inspire future athletes.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our operations and partnerships.

Community

We believe in the power of sports to unite communities and build lasting relationships across Zimbabwe.

Innovation

We continuously improve our platform with cutting-edge technology to serve our users better.

Sports Development in Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe has a rich sporting heritage, from football legends to cricket champions. At ZimSports League, we are committed to nurturing this legacy by providing the infrastructure needed for sports organizations to thrive.

Our platform supports multiple sports including:

  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Netball
  • Cricket
  • Rugby
  • Athletics
